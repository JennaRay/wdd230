Site Information

Site Name

Rexburg Chamber of Commerce

The name for the site will be straightforward and to the point, leaving no room for confusion or question. The straightforward name also gives the site a feeling of professionalism and taking its roll seriously.

Site Purpose

This site is a landing pad for business owners, and rexburg residents who want to know more about the local business community, as well as find resources and connections necessary for a thriving business community.

Scenarios

  • What do I need to do start my business here and how can I make it grow?
  • What resources and events does the Chamber of Commerce host to help the community?
  • How can I contact someone in the chamber of commerce?
